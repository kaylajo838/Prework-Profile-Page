About Me

My name is Kayla Imming. I was born and raised in Spokane, WA. I am raising two handsome boys. My family means everything to me. I have many different hobbies to keep me occupied in any spare time I have. Those hobbies include:

  • Cooking
  • Baking
  • Playing Video Games
  • Building Motorcycles
  • Building Race Cars
  • Going to Concerts
  • Photography
  • Modeling
  • Working Out
  • Rescuing/Rehabilitating Animals

I decided to I wanted to learn how to code after playing years of video games, and getting interested in wondering how the code worked to come together to build these games. I used to be interested in the medical field. I used to have aspirations to become a nurse. Before college, I decided to be a CNA to help my career path. After being around so much death and witnessing a lot of neglect, I decided on a career change. That was the best decision I could have made for myself. The complexity behind coding has my mind constantly turning, and has been exactly what I wanted it to be.
